List the ORBWEVA Pitch Night commands and what each does:

- `/pitch-night:build` — build the 3-minute spoken pitch script, budgeted second-by-second
- `/pitch-night:translate` — make your home market legible to judges who don't know it
- `/pitch-night:rehearse` — timing and delivery checklist against the clock
- `/pitch-night:score` — self-score against the judging rubric (cross-cultural clarity, positioning, GTM, market size, time discipline, proof)
- `/pitch-night:help` — this list

This skill is for a short spoken pitch to a cross-border audience. For an investor fundraising deck, use the `founder-pitch` skill instead.
