Run the ORBWEVA Pitch Night skill (`skills/pitch-night/SKILL.md`), Stage 4.

Score the current pitch 1–5 on each rubric dimension and report the total out of 30:

- Cross-cultural clarity — can a stranger picture the customer and problem in 30s without local knowledge?
- Positioning — what / who / why-different in one breath?
- Go-to-market — one named channel + why it fits this buyer?
- Market size — bottom-up count a foreigner can sanity-check, not top-down %?
- Time discipline — lands inside the limit on the ask?
- Proof — a real number or a built thing, not adjectives?

Total ≥ 26/30 is pitch-ready. Name the lowest two dimensions as the rewrite targets and say exactly what to change. Reference the full SKILL.md for the rubric.
